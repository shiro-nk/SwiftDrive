void sd.swiftclient.rk.gui.SubTaskController.refresh ( boolean  update)

Refresh Subtask Information
This function displays all the information given by the subtask to the screen. The checkboxes are enabled and disabled depending on the status of the subtask. For example, if the status is COMPLETE, the checbox "complete" will be checked and the "ignore" checkbox will be disabled (and unchecked).

This function also disables both checkboxes in the event that the task lead user is not the currently logged in user. Subtask statuses may only be modified by the user who is in charge of that subtask
